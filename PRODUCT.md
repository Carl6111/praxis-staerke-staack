# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Patients in Germany looking for a family doctor (Hausarzt). Context: deciding whether to register at this practice. Mobile-first, all age groups.

## Product Purpose

Marketing website for Hausarztpraxis Stärke & Staack. Present the practice, build trust, enable appointment booking via contact form. Secondary: show news/updates (Aktuelles page).

## Brand Personality

Warm, competent, human. Not sterile. Not luxury. Like a good doctor: you feel safe the moment you walk in.

## Anti-references

- Old German doctor websites (blue/white, stock photos, dated layout)
- Sterile hospital aesthetic
- SaaS/startup look (purple gradients, glassmorphism, hero metrics)

## Design Principles

1. Trust through warmth — every element should feel like it belongs in a calm, professional practice
2. Clarity over decoration — nothing confuses, nothing distracts from the key action (appointment booking)
3. Motion serves meaning — animations guide attention, never perform for their own sake
4. Mobile-first reality — most patients visit on phone, in a hallway or waiting room

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Reduced motion support required. Readable at all ages.
